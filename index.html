Entity 01 — The Program

RE-INCARNATION MUSIC ACADEMY

A 6–12 month Hip Hop Therapy program for artists ready to mine the story, sharpen the craft, and finish the album. Clinically informed. Culturally rooted. Outcome-measured with PHQ-9 and GAD-7 intake / midpoint / exit assessments.

PILLAR 01
Narrative Reconstruction
Cognitive Processing Therapy
Workbook-driven. Six chapters that mine the artist's full story arc — origin, darkest chapter, shadow, turning point, the build, the legacy. Every chapter feeds the album.
PILLAR 02
Identity & Legacy
Pro-Social Identity Therapy
Technical skill build across producing, engineering, performance, and digital presence. Artist leaves with a completed album AND a professional identity — author, not product of environment.
PILLAR 03
The Cypher
Therapeutic Community Model
The cohort. The circle. Group sessions, peer feedback, shared showcase moments. The Cypher replaces the gang — bonds form around creation, not criminality. Nobody builds alone in the NATiON.
